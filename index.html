Contracting With a Security Service Provider

Getting ready to procure manage service to help support or argument your security team? You are not alone: 62% of organisations said they plan to outsource some or all there IT securities functions in 2022 , according to the foundry . Before going down that root, it's wise to gather your requirements and think about the services you want from a managed security services provided(MSSP).

There are several basic consideration when choosing your service provider, including: the MSSPs experience, the types of support and the services they offer, and how there service levels agreement are structured. You'll also want to know the MSSPs specific domains of experties and how they correlate with your needs.
